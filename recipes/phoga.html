How to cook delicious chicken pho at home

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (approximately 1.5_2kg)
  • Chicken bones or marrow bones (for added sweetness)
  • Fresh rice noodles
  • Onion, ginger
  • Shallots
  • Seasonings: salt, fish sauce, rock sugar
  • Herbs: star anise, cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, coriander seeds
  • Side vegetables: scallions, cilantro, bean sprouts, lime, chili peppers

How to Cook Delicious Chicken Pho at Home

  • Step 1. Preparing the Ingredients

    Clean the chicken, rub with salt and ginger to remove any odor, then rinse with cold water. Briefly blanch the chicken and bones in boiling water for about 2-3 minutes to remove impurities, then rinse again. Roast the onion and ginger until slightly charred to create a characteristic aroma.

  • Step 2. Simmering the Broth The Secret to Deliciousness

    Put the chicken and bones in a large pot, add enough water to cover them, and bring to a boil. Once boiling, reduce the heat and frequently skim off the foam to keep the broth clear.

    Add the roasted onion and ginger along with the spice sachet (star anise, cinnamon, cardamom, etc.) that has been roasted until fragrant. Simmer for about 1-1.5 hours. When the chicken is cooked, remove it and soak it in cold water to make the skin crispy and prevent the meat from darkening.

  • Step 3. Seasoning

    Season with salt, fish sauce, and a little rock sugar to create a naturally sweet and savory taste. Don't add too many seasonings at once; season gradually to control the flavor.

  • Step 4. Prepare the chicken and rice noodles.

    Once the chicken has cooled, shred or chop it into bite-sized pieces. Quickly blanch the rice noodles in boiling water until soft and hot.

  • Step 5. Presentation and Enjoyment.

    Place the rice noodles in a bowl, arrange the chicken on top. Pour in the hot broth, sprinkle with chopped scallions and cilantro. Serve with lime, chili, and bean sprouts according to your preference.
